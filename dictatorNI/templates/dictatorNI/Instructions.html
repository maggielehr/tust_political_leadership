  • At the beginning of the first round of this task you will be randomly assigned the role of the decision maker or the role of the decision receiver.
    1. The decision maker begins the round with 100 points. The decision maker then decides how many of these points to send to the decision receiver. The remaining points are the payoff for the decision maker.
    2. The decision receiver begins the round with an endowment of zero points. The decision receiver's payoff is the amount sent to them by the decision maker.
  • The roles will be reversed for the second round and you will perform the action outlined in either 1) or 2) with the same partner.
    • Before the second round begins and the roles reversed, the decision receiver in the first round will not know how many points were allocated to them by the decision maker in the first round
  • After you have completed both rounds you will be prompted to continue to the second task of this session.
