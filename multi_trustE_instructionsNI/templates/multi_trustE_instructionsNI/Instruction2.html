The following multilateral distribution rounds will be structured as follows:

  1. Each participant will begin the round with 100 points. Each sender will independently decide how many of the points to send to the distributor. Each sender will also input the amount of points they expect to be returned from the distributor. If this number is within 10 points they will earn an additional 25 points for that round.
  2. The distributor will receive triple the summation of the points sent by each sender. For example, if each sender sends 2 points to the distributor, the distributor will receive 18 points. The summation of the sent points is 6 and 6x3 = 18.
  3. The distributor will decide how much of your available points you would like to keep for yourself
  4. You will then decide how you would like to redistribute the remainder of the allocated points. The distributor will see three boxes, the percentage of the remaining points the distributor would like to return to each sender should be inputted into the corresponding box for each sender. The total of these three boxes must equal 100%
  5. All senders will be notified of the amount of points returned to them. Senders will also know how many points the distributor kept. They will not know the amount of tokens returned to the other senders, nor the amount sent by the other senders to the distributor at the beginning of the round.
  6. At the end of the round a random number will be drawn between one and three.
    • If a one or two is drawn another round will be conducted with your same group repeating steps 1-4.
    • If a three is drawn groups will be reshuffled so that each player is placed randomly with three other participants. A random number generator will determine the single round from this group that is selected for payment. You will not know what number was selected for payment until all groups have completed play.
  7. After groups have been reshuffled steps 1-4 are repeated with the new group until a three is drawn again. Each distributor will only play in the role of the distributor for one group. At this point groups will be shuffled again and you will repeat steps 1-4 with your final group.
  8. After the final group play has finished due to a random draw of the number 3, the round selected for payment from each group will be displayed and your total take-home earnings will be calculated. Your participation fee will be included in this value.



Please note that each round is independent so points do not transfer between rounds.
