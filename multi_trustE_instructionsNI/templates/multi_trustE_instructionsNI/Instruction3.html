Earnings

Your earnings from this task will be from two randomly selected rounds.
  • The amount of points the distributor earns from the selected round is dependent on how many points the distributors keep from the senders' allocations.
  • The amount of points each sender earns is the points each sender began the round with and did not sent to the distributor plus the amount sent to them by the distributor.
    • An additional 25 points if the sender's estimation of the amount returned from the distributor was within 10 points of the actual amount of returned points.
  • The monetary earnings from this round will be converted at a ratio of 25 points to $1.00 USD. Cash earnings will be rounded to the nearest US dollar.
